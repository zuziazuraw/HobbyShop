Camping Gear

Shelter

Bringing a shelter is fundamental when going camping. You need to bring a tent and sleeping bag to stay warm and dry.

Fire

Bring a lighter, fire starter or flint as a source of heat, a way to cook food and in case of emergencies.

Food and Water

Its essential that you bring food and water for the camping trip. Bring healthy snacks so you can have lots of energy.
